About This Site
🎯 Our Goal
🦷
Helping Patients Find the Right Clinic
The goal of this website is to help patients easily find clinics that fits their dental needs. The website aims to make the process of choosing a clinic more convenient to the public by providing the clinic's location, and contact details.
🔬
Science, Technology, and Society
In connection with Science, Technology, and Society, DMD 1-A utilized technology as a tool to improve access to oral care services to the community. This website demonstrates how science and technology can be applied to everyday challenges and provide people service by making dental information and services easier to reach.
